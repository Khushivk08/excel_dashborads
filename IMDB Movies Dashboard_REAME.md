# IMDB Movies Dashboard

![IMDB Movies Dashboard Screenshot](images/IMDB%MOVIES.png)

## 📘 Overview
The **IMDB Movies Dashboard** visualizes movie industry data including genre, rating, director profits, and global revenue distribution.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Identify profitable directors and high-grossing genres

- Track revenue trends by release year and country

- Compare movie ratings and genre popularity

## 📊 Key Insights

- **Top director:** James Cameron  

- **Leading genre:** Action & Adventure  

- **Revenue leader:** USA ($1.34E+11 total revenue)

- **Peak years:** 2005–2006 show highest gross revenue

## ⚙️ Filters
- Rating, Country, Genre, Year of Release

## 🧭 Tools Used

- Excel Pivot Charts  

- Map visualization for country revenue  

- Interactive slicers for filtering
