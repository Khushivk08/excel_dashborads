# Adidas Sales Dashboard

![Adidas Sales Dashboard Screenshot](ADDIDAS%SALES.png)

## 📘 Overview
The **Adidas Sales Dashboard** provides a comprehensive view of Adidas’s sales performance across years, regions, product categories, and retailers. It visualizes total sales, operating profit, and sales methods using interactive charts and filters.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Track monthly and yearly sales trends (2020–2021)

- Compare retailer performance by total sales and operating profit

- Analyze regional and product-wise distribution

- Understand sales method contribution (In-store, Online, Outlet)

## 📊 Key Insights

- **Peak sales:** May & November 2021  

- **Top retailer:** West Gear ($85,567.87K)  

- **Regional leader:** California ($157M)  

- **Sales method split:** In-store (40%), Outlet (33%), Online (27%)

## ⚙️ Filters

- Years: 2020, 2021  

- Product: Men’s & Women’s Apparel, Athletic & Street Footwear  

- Region: Midwest, Northeast, South, Southeast, West  

## 🧭 Tools Used

- Excel Pivot Charts  

- Map Visualization  

- Slicers for dynamic filtering
