# Sweetmart Sales Dashboard

![Sweetmart Sales Dashboard Screenshot](images/SWEETMART%SALES.png)

## 📘 Overview
The **Sweetmart Sales Dashboard** tracks sales performance across customers, regions, delivery personnel, and food items.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Identify top customers and food items

- Analyze regional sales distribution and delivery efficiency

- Monitor monthly and yearly sales trends

## 📊 Key Insights
- **Top customer:** C299 ($4,492.49)

- **Top food item:** Balushahi ($32,020.92)

- **Leading region:** South East (30.24% of total sales)

- **Sales peak:** Mid-2021 shows highest monthly sales

## ⚙️ Filters

- Years (2021–2023)

- Months (Jan–Apr)

- Region and Food Item

## 🧭 Tools Used

- Excel Pivot Charts  

- Pie and bar charts  

- Slicers for dynamic filtering
